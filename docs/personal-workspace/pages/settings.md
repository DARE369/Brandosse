# Personal Page Concept: Settings

Updated: 2026-05-18

## Purpose

I am thinking about Settings as the control area for personal account readiness.

The most important job of Settings is to help the user connect and maintain the social accounts they publish to.

## What It Should Help With

Settings should help the user:

- Connect personal social accounts.
- Reconnect expired accounts.
- Disconnect accounts.
- See account health.
- Understand publishing readiness.
- View organization-shared accounts separately when relevant.

## Main User Questions

Settings should answer:

- Which accounts are connected?
- Which accounts need attention?
- Can I publish to this account?
- Is this account personal or organization-shared?
- What should I fix before publishing?

## Ideal Flow

The user opens Settings, connects or fixes accounts, and returns to Generate or Calendar with confidence that publishing can work.

## Success Outcome

Settings succeeds if the user's connected accounts are clear, healthy, and ready for publishing.
