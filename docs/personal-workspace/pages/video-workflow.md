# Personal Page Concept: Video Workflow

Updated: 2026-05-18

## Purpose

I am thinking about the video workflow as a dedicated space for longer video creation jobs.

Some video tasks may take more time and credits than quick image generation, so the user needs a clear place to submit, track, and review them.

## Video Pages

The video workflow can include:

- New video page.
- Video jobs page.
- Video job detail page.

## New Video Page

The New Video page should help the user submit a video creation request.

It should make clear:

- What the user is asking to create.
- How many credits are available.
- What inputs are needed.
- What happens after submission.

## Video Jobs Page

The Video Jobs page should help the user track active and completed jobs.

It should answer:

- What is still processing?
- What is complete?
- What failed?
- Which job should I open?

## Video Job Detail Page

The Video Job Detail page should show the details of one job and its outputs.

It should help the user:

- Preview completed clips.
- Understand job status.
- Access outputs.
- Return to the jobs list.

## Success Outcome

The video workflow succeeds if long-running video work feels trackable instead of hidden or uncertain.
