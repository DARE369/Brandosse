# Personal Page Concept: Billing and Credits

Updated: 2026-05-18

## Purpose

I am thinking about Billing and Credits as the place where users understand usage, especially for heavier generation workflows like video.

## What It Should Help With

This page should help the user see:

- Current credit balance.
- Credit usage history.
- Recent transactions.
- Whether they have enough credits to continue.
- When more credits may be needed.

## Main User Questions

Billing and Credits should answer:

- How many credits do I have?
- What used my credits?
- Can I start another video or generation job?
- Do I need to top up?

## Ideal Flow

The user checks credit balance before or after a credit-heavy action, understands what changed, and decides whether to continue or add more credits.

## Success Outcome

Billing and Credits succeeds if usage feels transparent and limits do not surprise the user.
